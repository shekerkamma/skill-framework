# Expected Results

## current-topic

Expected characteristics:
- mentions current dates or recent time markers
- cites official docs, repos, or first-party pages where possible
- avoids generic timeless claims about a fast-moving space

## multi-view-topic

Expected characteristics:
- benefits and risks both represented
- consensus and debate clearly separated
- no one-sided advocacy disguised as research

## uncertain-topic

Expected characteristics:
- uncertainty is explicit
- direct evidence and inference are distinguished
- limits of evidence are stated clearly
