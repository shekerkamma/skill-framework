# Documentation Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing a technical document.

## Audience

- Is the target reader clear?
- Is the reader's goal explicit?
- Is the document pitched at the right level?

## Accuracy

- Are technical claims supported by source material?
- Are commands, paths, and examples consistent?
- Are assumptions labeled as assumptions?

## Structure

- Does the document type have the right section order?
- Is the quick start early enough?
- Are headings descriptive?

## Clarity

- Are paragraphs short and focused?
- Is jargon defined when needed?
- Are examples useful instead of decorative?

## Utility

- Can the reader take a first action quickly?
- Are common failure modes addressed?
- Are next steps clear?
