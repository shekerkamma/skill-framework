# Research Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing a research output.

## Scoping

- Is the question clearly defined?
- Is the required depth clear?
- Are the key subtopics identified?

## Source Quality

- Are the strongest claims backed by primary or authoritative sources?
- Are publication dates appropriate for the topic?
- Are weak or biased sources treated carefully?

## Time Sensitivity

- Were unstable facts checked against current sources?
- Are exact dates included where they matter?

## Synthesis

- Are findings grouped into themes?
- Are consensus and disagreement separated?
- Are gaps or uncertainties stated clearly?

## Citation Quality

- Do citations map to actual claims?
- Are sources listed with enough detail to revisit them?

## Output Quality

- Is the summary decision-relevant?
- Is the confidence level proportional to the evidence?
- Are inferences labeled instead of implied as fact?
